About Dr. Escobar

Meet Dr. D. Escobar

Dr. D. Escobar started in a single converted cottage with one doctor, one nurse, and a waiting list of neighbours. Today we're a full family practice — but the promise hasn't changed: real appointment time, honest answers, and care that follows you through every stage of life.

We believe good healthcare feels less like a transaction and more like a relationship. That's why our doctors keep smaller patient panels, our nurses know your kids by name, and our follow-ups actually follow up.
